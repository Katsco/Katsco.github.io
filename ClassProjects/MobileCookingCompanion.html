Mobile Cooking Companion
< / >

This class project, the Mobile Cooking Companion (MCC), aimed to assist the way average household cook
went about figuring out what to cook with the ingedients they have without resulting in unused food waste.
As the team lead, I spearheaded a dynamic group of talented individuals who shared a passion for technology
and enhancing the average experience of a homecook.

In this project, we worked to propose, plan, design, implement, and test a software application.
